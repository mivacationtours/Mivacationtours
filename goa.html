Goa Packages



Package 1

4 nights & 5 days Package :

Days Tour Details
Day1 Arrive in Goa, On arrival at the Goa Airport/Railway station. You will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. Day at leisure. Overnight at Hotel.
Day2Goa After Breakfast, Start for Goa Full Day Sightseeing, Visit River Cruise, Calangute Beaches, Fort Aguada, Light House Point, Aguada Beach, Vagator Beach, Anjuna Beach, Harbour Point, Bogmalo Beach, Old Goa Church, Shri Manguesh Temple, Shri Shantadurga Temple, Colva Beach, etc., as per available timeSpend the evening at leisure or at the beach. Overnight at Hotel.
Day3Goa Day at Leisure at the beach OR take one of our below suggested tours.Castaway Cruise A day out on a boat and around islands with dolphins for company. Set sail from Vainguinim Beach, ride the waves into the Arabian Sea, then proceed to Bogmallo Beach and then past Bat Island. The spray of the sea, the sun on your face, and clear waters are sheer bliss. Keep a look out for Dolphins who may put on a show for you.Drop anchor at Grande Island for a spot of fishing, snorkel to keep company with marine life or just laze away the day. A leisurely lunch on mainland, some more sunbathing and the sail back home.Riveredge Adventure Enjoy one full day out of Goa at the beautiful river Kali. Experience the peaceful location filled with a portion adventure. Go for a short trekking, climb rocks, go for a swim, for canoeing, Banana ride etc. or just relax surrounded by Nature. Overnight at Hotel.
Day4Goa Breakfast at the hotel and depart for the South Goa tour, includes CALANGUTE TO MIRAMAR A short but sweet beach, here lies the Samadhi of Goa’s First Chief Minister Lata Dayananda Bandodkar.DONA PAULA From its high point can be seen Zuari meeting Arabian Sea. BOM JESUS OF BASILICA ( Old Goa Churches ) Where lies the remains of St. Xavier’s Body.St. CATHEDRAL The largest of its kind in the East. MANGUESHI TEMPLE Dedicated to Lord Shiva, being built in the 17th Century. SHANTADURGA TEMPLE One of the richest temples dedicated to Shanthadurga the Goddess of peace also famous for golden palanquin. COLVA BEACH Famous as the longest beach in Goa, only rival to Calangute in popularity. RIVER CRUIZE While you watch the lively culture of Goa in song and dance.
Day5Goa After a leisurely breakfast, to drive goa visit local sightseeing. Later our representative will transfer you to airport/Railway station to board your return flight/train back to your own destination.
