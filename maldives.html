Maldive Packages



Package 1

4 Nights & 5 Days Maldives Package :

Days Tour Details
Day1 Arrive in Maldives : Arrive at the airport in Male and transfer to Resort. Later, board the speed boat to reach the resort (45 min approx.). As soon as you arrive, check in at the resort. (Check-in time is 01:00 PM.) Enjoy your overnight stay at the resort. Lunch and dinner facilities will be included.
Day2 Resort - Enjoy Water Activities : After Breakfast enjoy water activities at the resort. Indulge in exciting activities such as catamaran sailing, windsurfing, snorkelling and diving. All activities are on direct payment basis. Overnight stay facility will be arranged at the resort. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are included.
Day3Resort : After breakfast, day free for leisure activities, lunch and dinner are included. Overnight stay at resort.
Day4Resort Departure : Check out from resort and board the speedboat to reach airport (45 minutes). Only breakfast is included. Once you reach the airport if you have time you can go for Male city tour which includes The Fish Market and The Local Market, Friday Mosque, The Sultan Park, The National Museum, The Islamic Centre, Tomb of Abul Barakat Al Barbary, The Presidential Palace, The President's Office etc.
